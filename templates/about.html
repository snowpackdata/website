Why Snowpack?

Data in Layers

We believe the strongest data stacks are supported by well-defined layers of data ingestion, processing, and reporting that establish trust throughout the organization. Our name Snowpack Data was derived from the process of avalanche forecasters in assessing the stability of snow. We bring this process to our customers, ensuring that each layer supports the next, and that weak layers don't bring the whole stack down.

We offer an efficient option for companies looking to jump-start their analytics organization or provide key support to existing teams. We are able to independently deliver on key projects and provide a roadmap for future work that your team can build on.

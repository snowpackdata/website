Data in Layers

We view a proper data stack has composed of important layers. Each required before to support and unlock the value of the next. Snowpack Data specializes in structuring a scalable foundation your analytics org.

  • Raw Data

    Ingestion and Loading

    The first step in building your foundation for analytics is ensuring data is loaded and stored correctly as the raw layer in your data lake.

  • Base Models

    Structuring your key data models

    With solid core data you can focus on developing key base models. These models will form the backbone of your analytics layer. With well structured base models your analytics earn trust across the business.

  • Entity Modeling

    Expose High Value Data Endpoints

    Using your base models you can now begin to model entities, complex tables of businesss logic that deliver a simple interface to business users who need to interpret data.

  • Metrics Layer

    Measuring KPIs

    Using your entities models you can move into the holy grail of analytics, a self-propogating metrics layer. Easily generate key business metrics and provide precise measurement on what matters to your org.
